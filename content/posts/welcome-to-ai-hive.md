Welcome to **AI Hive** — a personal blog where I write about artificial intelligence, machine learning, and research.

## What this blog is about

I'm building this as a living notebook. The goal is simple: write clearly about things I'm learning, so that I actually understand them — and so others can benefit too.

Expect posts on:

- **Research paper breakdowns** — translating dense papers into plain English
- **ML concepts** — intuition-first explanations of algorithms and architectures
- **Projects** — experiments I'm running, what worked, what didn't
- **AI news & ideas** — commentary on what's happening in the field
